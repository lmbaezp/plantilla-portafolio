Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services, development process, and how I can help bring your project to life.

1. What technologies do you use for web and mobile development?

I use modern technologies such as React, Vue.js, and Angular for front-end development, and Node.js, Django, and Laravel for back-end development. For mobile applications, I work with React Native and Flutter to create cross-platform solutions.

2. How long does it take to develop a website or application?

The development time depends on the project's complexity and requirements. A simple website can take 1-2 weeks, while a complex web application may take several months. After discussing your needs, I can provide a more accurate timeline.

3. Do you offer website maintenance and optimization services?

Yes! I provide website maintenance, performance optimization, and security updates to ensure your website remains fast, secure, and up to date. I also offer ongoing support to implement new features as needed.

4. Can you integrate third-party services and APIs into my project?

Absolutely! I can integrate various third-party services, including payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal), authentication providers (Google, Facebook login), cloud storage, and external APIs to enhance your application's functionality.

5. Do you work with Agile methodologies like Scrum?

Yes, I have experience implementing Agile methodologies and working as a Scrum Master. I can help manage projects using Scrum practices, ensuring efficient collaboration, regular updates, and timely delivery.
